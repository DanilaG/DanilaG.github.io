Doji

Dpji is a reliable and intuitive tool for recognizing candle patterns on the Russian stock market. The application is intended for investors, traders and anyone interested in forecasting stock movements and making informed decisions.

The application uses advanced algorithms for recognizing and analyzing candlestick patterns to identify key patterns on the charts of Russian companies' stocks. Which in turn helps to identify buy and sell signals, as well as to assess the future direction of the price.
